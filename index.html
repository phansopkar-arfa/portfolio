I'm a Computer Science Engineer pursuing B.E. in CSE (AI & ML) with Honours in Data Science from Mumbai University.

My journey in technology began at Government Polytechnic Ratnagiri where I completed my Diploma in Computer Engineering. This strong foundation led me to Finolex Academy of Management and Technology where I'm now specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

I specialize in creating end-to-end solutions — from predictive ML pipelines and NLP-powered recruitment tools to full-stack AI web applications. My flagship project, the AI Career Companion, is a Django platform integrating Groq LLM for real-time mock interviews, ATS resume scoring, and career coaching — all built and deployed by me.
